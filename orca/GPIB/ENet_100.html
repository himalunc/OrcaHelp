GPIB ENet/100 Controller

 
A GPIB-Enet Box is needed in a configuration to communicate between the computer (host) and any GPIB objects (ie. Scopes, Pulsers).


Due to legal concerns we can not make available the National Instruments drivers. ORCA will now build just fine without you having the NI driver, but the ENet/100 controller will not work, the controls will be disabled, and the icon will be X’ed out. Please contact National Instruments to obtain the driver.


See the user’s manual for information about setting this device up.
