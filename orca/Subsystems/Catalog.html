Object Catalog

 
To use ORCA you must make design a configuration of objects that matches your needs. All of the objects that ORCA supports are in the object catalog. Open the object Catalog from the Windows->Catalog or click on the catalog icon at the top of the configuration window. Here is what the catalog looks like, with an overview of how to use it:
 

Double-click objects to open their dialogs

Drag objects from the catalog into the config window

VME card objects can only be dragged into VME crates. Other objects may have similar constraints on where they can be dropped.
