LakeShore 210

 
The Model 210 is an eight input temperature monitor that can be used with diode or resistive temperature sensors. The measurement input was designed for the demands of cryogenic temperature measurement. The monitor’s low noise, high resolution and wide operating range make it ideal for non cryogenic applications as well. 


See the manual for more info.
