Drop zones for controller cards


The icon can have various numbers of connectors associated with the object depending on which PCI cards are being used and can be distinguished by their color. The FireWire connector is always present. Restrictions are in place to prevent connectors from being connected to incompatible objects. In general, just follow the color coding.
